Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich and hearty meat sauce, perfect for family dinners. This recipe is simple enough for beginners but delicious enough to impress anyone.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Reduce heat and let simmer for 30 minutes to allow flavors to develop.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Drain pasta, then top with the Bolognese sauce.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with Parmesan cheese.

Tips

For a richer flavor, you can add a splash of red wine while the sauce simmers. If you want a smoother texture, blend the sauce before serving.
